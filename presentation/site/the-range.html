Bold, polished and designed to stand out. Our statement candles are poured into striking metal containers, available in warm copper, bright silver and textured dimpled finishes. Signature Fragrance Development

Developing a completely custom scent is a beautiful process, best suited to high-volume collections. Working with fragrance houses to blend something entirely new requires a larger scale to be viable. For independent brands and boutique orders, our existing library is the perfect starting point — with over 100 developed fragrances ready to go, we can help you find a scent that feels entirely your own.
