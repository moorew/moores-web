Who we are

30 years of design.
15 years of scent.

Moores Home & Garden is a dedicated fragrance workshop in the Sussex countryside founded by Terence and Suzy Moore. Drawing on 30 years of high-end interior design experience, we hand-pour candles, diffusers, and home fragrance for luxury retailers, hospitality brands, and corporate clients.

We are not a factory, and we are not a catalogue. We are a team of makers who care how a room feels when your guests walk into it — and who can craft that feeling for orders of any size.

— Terence, Suzy, and the Moores team
