Moores Home & Garden is a dedicated fragrance workshop in the Sussex countryside, founded by Terence and Suzy Moore — drawing on three decades of high-end interior design.

Before fragrance

Four decades in design.

Prior to founding the workshop, Terence and Suzy ran a highly successful interior and floral design business for 30 years. Terence, the creative mind behind the brand, has authored eight books on design and brought his vision to some of the most recognised names in retail and public life.

His portfolio includes interior consulting for Harrods, Marks & Spencer, and members of the Royal Family, alongside custom product ranges developed for figures like Laurence Llewelyn-Bowen and Rylan Clark. His bold, unique designs have been featured across television and international magazines for over four decades.

How it began

From spaces
to scent.

After three decades of designing exceptional physical spaces, Terence and Suzy realised that scent is the final, most lasting element of any room. They pivoted their design expertise entirely into home fragrance, starting at a kitchen table — pouring a few candles for friends and local stockists.

The fragrances stood out, and those stockists kept asking for more. Fifteen years on, we are a capable team working from a custom workshop in the Sussex countryside. We handle large commercial orders, but we keep the kitchen-table care. Everything is blended in-house, poured by hand in small batches, and finished by the same people who made it.

How we make it

Slow, by hand, in small batches.

Blended in-house

We develop and blend our own fragrances — over a hundred of them — rather than buying them in ready-made.

Poured by hand

Every candle is hand-poured to order in small batches, so nothing sits on a shelf losing its scent.

European glassware

Our glass comes from trusted makers across Europe — weighty, clear, and made to be kept and refilled.

Finished & checked

Boxed, trimmed, and ribboned by the same people who poured it. If it is not right, it does not leave.
