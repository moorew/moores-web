01The shift

The problem was never the product. It was the noise.

The old site tried to show everything at once — every range, every scent, every page. Dated, hard to update, and it buried the one thing that wins work: a conversation.

The old site

  • × Ten-plus menu items; every product listed
  • × Ageing WordPress build, due for renewal
  • × Outdated content nobody could easily change
  • × No clear next step for a serious buyer
  • × No coherent palette, type or voice

The new brand

  • → Five lean pages — who they are, the range hinted
  • → One job: request a consultation
  • → A simple, low-maintenance modern build
  • → Warm, luxurious, unmistakably handmade
  • → A real identity: colour, type, mark & voice

02Positioning

A Sussex fragrance house — for homes and for brands.

Moores sits in a rare middle ground: a genuine hand-poured maker that also produces under other people's names. Not a catalogue — a workshop you can commission.

The one-line promise

“Hand-poured candles & fragrance, made properly in Sussex — for your home, or under your name.”

04Colour

Warm quiet luxury.
